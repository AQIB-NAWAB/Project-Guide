## Learning log — mandatory

Create **`learning-log/03-why-mongodb.md`**. Answer in **complete sentences**. Reference **your product name** and **your ERD**.

### Questions

1. **Why MongoDB for your marketplace?** Give two reasons tied to this project (grocery fields, order embeds, JSON API, etc.) — not generic NoSQL slogans.

2. **Steelman SQL:** What is the **strongest** argument for PostgreSQL for this same product? Why does the course still use MongoDB?

3. **Embed vs reference:** Walk through checkout — what is embedded on the order document, what is referenced, and what gets copied into the snapshot at the moment of purchase?

4. **Denormalisation:** Explain why copying `vendorName` onto every product is dangerous but copying `priceAtPurchase` onto an order line is correct.

5. **Actor isolation:** How does your ERD ensure Vendor A cannot update Vendor B's product? Where is the enforcement point (DB vs application)?

6. **Open question:** One modelling decision you left open for Chapter 5 — what is it and how will you decide?

7. **Diagram:** Paste your Mermaid or link to your diagram file inside the learning log.
