# 15.9 — Product detail page

**Why this step matters:** Customers decide to buy on the **detail** page — larger photo, full description, store name, and the add-to-cart button stub Chapter 17 will wire. Without detail, the marketplace is a window with no door.

**Where the project is now:** Shop grid links to `/shop/:productId` but detail may be placeholder.

**After this step:** Detail fetches `GET /products/:id`, displays full info, **Add to cart** button stub for Chapter 17.
