## Decisions you can defend in a viva

1. **Why one store per vendor?** — Simplifies cart split, ownership, onboarding.

2. **Why 409 on second POST?** — Valid auth, business rule violation — not 403.

3. **Why GET /stores/me?** — Token-scoped; no client-supplied vendor id.

4. **Why 403 not 404 on cross-vendor PATCH?** — Consistent with Chapter 8 IDOR tests.

5. **Why vendorId not in request body?** — Prevents privilege escalation.
