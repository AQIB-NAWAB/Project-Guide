## Viva-ready answers

1. **Why split orders?** — Per-vendor fulfilment; isolation; marketplace model.
2. **Why snapshots?** — Historical truth; price edits must not rewrite receipts.
3. **Why conditional $inc?** — Prevent oversell under concurrency.
4. **Why clear cart after success?** — Prevent duplicate checkout from stale cart.
