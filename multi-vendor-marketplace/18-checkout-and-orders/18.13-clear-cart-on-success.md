# 18.13 — Clear cart on success

**Why this step matters:** Without clearing cart after successful checkout, customers can **double-order** the same lines with one more POST — empty cart is part of the checkout contract, not optional cleanup.

**Where the project is now:** Orders created and stock decremented; cart may still hold lines — customer could checkout again.

**After this step:** Successful checkout **deletes all cart items** for the customer atomically with order creation.

---

## Clear contract

After orders inserted and stock decremented:

```
await CartItem.deleteMany({ userId })
```

Or your cart module's `clearCart(userId)` service function — reuse cart service, don't duplicate query logic.

**Only on full success** — if checkout threw before commit, cart untouched.

---

## Transaction ordering

Preferred inside single transaction:

1. Decrement stock (all lines)
2. Insert orders
3. Delete cart items
4. Commit

Customer never sees empty cart without orders.

---

## Response after clear

`GET /cart` immediately after checkout → empty items array.

Client Chapter 19 should refetch cart or zero badge after checkout success.

---

## Double checkout prevention (MVP)

With cart cleared, second immediate `POST /checkout` → `400` empty cart. Not full idempotency — see 18.6 — but prevents duplicate from same cart state.

---

## ✅ Do / ❌ Don't

**✅ Do:** Clear by `userId` — all lines, all stores.

**✅ Do:** Call from checkout service — not client DELETE loop.

**❌ Don't:** Clear cart before orders persisted.

**❌ Don't:** Leave cart items when returning 201.
