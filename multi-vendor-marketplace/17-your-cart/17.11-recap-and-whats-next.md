## Decisions you can defend in a viva

1. **Why group by store in UI?** — Same boundary as multi-vendor checkout split (Ch 18).
2. **Why server cart not localStorage?** — Persistence across devices; auth scoping; source of truth.
3. **Why refetch after PATCH?** — Avoid drift; server enforces stock rules.
4. **Cart prices vs order prices?** — Cart shows live catalogue prices; orders snapshot at checkout.
