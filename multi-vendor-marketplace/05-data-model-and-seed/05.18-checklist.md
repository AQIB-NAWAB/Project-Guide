4. **User vs Profile:** What lives on User vs Profile in your implementation? What would break if merged into one document?

5. **Seed inspection:** What did you see in Compass that surprised you — collection names, field types, or counts?

6. **ERD diff:** List two differences between erd-draft.md and final erd.md. Why did you change them?

7. **Idempotency:** What strategy does your seed use? What happened when you ran it twice?
